PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: June 1, 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 1, 2025

THE SHORT VERSION Arcane Ledger does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. Everything you enter — player names, game history, settings — stays on your device and never leaves it. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no ads.

1. Who We Are

Arcane Ledger is an independent mobile application developed by Syberdesign. It is a game tracking tool for use with tabletop card games. Arcane Ledger is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Wizards of the Coast LLC.

For questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@syberdesign.com

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Location data of any kind
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Camera, microphone, or contacts access
  • Any data transmitted over the internet

Arcane Ledger operates entirely offline. The app makes no network requests during normal operation.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

Arcane Ledger stores the following data locally on your device only, using your device's native storage system:

  • Player names you enter during game setup
  • Game history records (final standings and match logs)
  • Whether you have previously seen the introduction screen

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any server. It can be deleted at any time by clearing the app's data through your device settings, or by uninstalling the app.

We do not have access to this data. It belongs entirely to you.

4. Third-Party Services

Arcane Ledger uses Google Fonts to load display typefaces. When the app is first loaded, your device may make a request to Google's font servers (fonts.googleapis.com and fonts.gstatic.com) to download font files. This is a standard web font request; Arcane Ledger does not control or receive any data from this interaction. Google's privacy policy governs their handling of font requests and can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

No other third-party services, SDKs, advertising networks, or analytics tools are used in Arcane Ledger.

5. Children's Privacy

Arcane Ledger does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no features that require or encourage the entry of personal information, and no data is transmitted off-device. The app is rated 4+ and is appropriate for all ages.

6. Data Security

Because Arcane Ledger does not transmit or store data on external servers, there is no risk of a data breach involving your information from our end. Data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security measures (passcode, biometric lock, etc.).

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or hold any of your personal data, there is nothing for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. All data the app stores is on your own device and fully within your control.

To delete all app data: go to your device's Settings → Apps → Arcane Ledger → Clear Data, or simply uninstall the application.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy — for example, if a future version of Arcane Ledger introduces online features — we will update the effective date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users through an in-app notice.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Arcane Ledger's data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@syberdesign.com

We will respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.
